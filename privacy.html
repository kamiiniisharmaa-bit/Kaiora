Privacy Pledge

At Kaiōra, we honor the trust you place in us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information — because transparency is the foundation of mindful commerce.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Our Commitment to You

Kaiōra (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website kaiora.com (the “Site”). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use it, your rights, and the choices you have about your data. By using our Site or purchasing our artisanal products, you agree to the practices described herein.

Our core belief: Your data belongs to you. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a seamless, secure, and personalized shopping experience:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, billing/shipping address, phone number — provided when you create an account, place an order, or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details or PayPal credentials are processed by secure third-party payment processors (Stripe, PayPal). Kaiōra never stores full payment card numbers.
  • Order History: Details of products purchased, returns, and preferences to improve our offerings.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies (see Section 6) for site functionality and analytics.
  • Communication Data: Any correspondence you send to us via email, chat, or social media.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders (shipping, payment confirmation, returns).
  • Sending essential service communications (order updates, delivery status).
  • Improving our Site, products, and customer service through analytics.
  • Sending marketing emails (only if you opt in) — featuring new artisan collections, seasonal stories, and exclusive offers. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Preventing fraud and enhancing security of our platform.
  • Complying with legal obligations (tax, anti-fraud regulations).

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal data. However, we share information with trusted partners who help us operate:

  • Shipping carriers (USPS, DHL, FedEx) to deliver your order.
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) to handle transactions securely.
  • Email service provider (Klaviyo) to manage newsletters — only for opted-in users.
  • Analytics partners (Google Analytics, Fathom) to improve user experience without collecting identifiable personal data beyond IP masking).

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for specified services.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance site navigation, analyze usage, and remember your preferences. You can control cookies via browser settings.

Essential Cookies
Necessary for checkout, security, and cart.
Preference Cookies
Remember language & region.
Analytics Cookies
Anonymous usage data (Google Analytics).

By continuing to use our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described. You may disable non-essential cookies via our cookie banner at first visit.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Typically:

  • Order data: 7 years (tax compliance).
  • Newsletter subscription data: until you unsubscribe.
  • Customer account data: until you request deletion.

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, firewalls, access controls) to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU residents, CCPA for California), you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell data; however, you may opt-out of marketing emails via unsubscribe link.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kaiora.com. We will respond within 30 days.

GDPR & CCPA compliance: If you are a resident of the European Economic Area or California, additional disclosures apply — please see our full legal notice or contact our Data Protection Officer.

8. Children's Privacy

Kaiōra does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 years of age. Our products are intended for adults. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians may contact us to request removal.

9. External Links

Our Site may contain links to external websites (e.g., artisan profiles, payment gateways). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when changes were made. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on our Site.
